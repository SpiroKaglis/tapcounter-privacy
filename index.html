TapCounter Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 11, 2025

Introduction

TapCounter (referred to as "we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains our practices regarding data collection and usage for our TapCounter mobile application.

Information We Don't Collect

TapCounter is a simple counter application that operates entirely on your device. We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information, usage data, or any other type of data from your device. The app functions completely offline and does not require internet connectivity.

Third-Party Services

TapCounter does not integrate with any third-party services, analytics tools, or advertising networks. We do not share any information with third parties because we do not collect any information.

Children's Privacy

TapCounter is suitable for users of all ages and does not collect any personal information from anyone, including children under the age of 13.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.
